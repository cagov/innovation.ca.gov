We’re excited to congratulate Jeffery Marino and JP Petrucione on their new roles at the Office of Digital Innovation (ODI).

Jeffery is now our Deputy Director of Service Innovation. He previously served as ODI’s Insights Program Manager.

JP is now our Deputy Director of User Research. He was formerly ODI’s Deputy Director of Communications.

User research and service innovation are critical parts of ODI’s mission. These appointments position ODI to deliver better services through human-centered design and technology.

Governor Gavin Newsom appointed Jeffery and JP to these roles on April 20, 2022. They are part of a leadership team headed by Amy Tong. Governor Newsom named her Director of ODI in December 2021. She is now Secretary of the Government Operations Agency.
