California Work Opportunity and Responsibility to Kids (CalWORKs) is a major safety net program. A recent study estimated 40% of eligible families are not enrolled in CalWORKs. The Office of Data and Innovation (ODI) worked with the Department of Social Services to explore enrollment barriers.

We learned that:

  • Reduced burdens will improve enrollment and retention
  • Burdens to applicants and enrollees are also burdens to county staff
  • Communication to applicants and enrollees needs to be more clear, timely, and streamlined
  • Simpler policy guidance to counties would ease administrative burden

Learn more about the details of helping more eligible families enroll in CalWORKs.
