Why you should choose ODI

  • We’re passionate about making a difference in the lives of Californians. We take the responsibility of knowing how our work affects real people seriously.
  • You’ll get to work with amazing people who care deeply about improving their communities.
  • We’re testing cool new tools and ways of work. We explore new trends to figure out how they can help the rest of the state.
  • State employment offers stability and great benefits. But we’re not your average state department. We’re expanding the possibilities for what state departments can do.
  • We’re a fully remote workplace. Most positions can work from anywhere in California. (But if you’re in Sacramento, we’ve got an office if you ever want to come in.)
