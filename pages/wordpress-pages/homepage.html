Shaping California, one conversation at a time

ODI is proud to introduce Engaged California, an innovative new program designed to foster meaningful dialogue and strengthen trust between Californians and their government. The first use case of the program is focused on survivors of the LA firestorm and those who have been impacted by the recovery.
