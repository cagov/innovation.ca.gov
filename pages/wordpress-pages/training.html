The Office of Data and Innovation can help you improve your innovation skills. We offer self-paced and real-time training that help you serve Californians better.

Self-paced training

You can take online training at your own pace for free. Most are available through CalLearns.

  • Plain language for the public sector: Learn how to write so everyone can understand and why it helps
  • Building better services with plain language: Gain new plain language skills, including pair writing
  • Introduction to product approach for the public sector: A self-paced course that provides an introduction to using product principles in government
  • Human-centered design: A self-paced course focused on building human-centered design skills

Group training for departments

We offer live training for departments in:

  • Human-centered design
  • Content design and plain language
