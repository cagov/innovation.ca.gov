  • Project manage client projects

At each stage you will lead on client engagement, serving as a full partner in the analytic process. You will work with the team to think up and test analytical and evaluation approaches to best meet the business need. You will conduct user research to ensure the success of any analytic products developed. You will roll up your sleeves and help out on analytics.

Our solicitation model is based in part on successful work existing team members did in San Francisco with DataScienceSF. You will thoughtfully pilot, modify, and iterate on this prior foundation for the state context. You will flex your strategic thinking muscles to:

  • Analyze the service as a whole and the organization context of the state
  • Identify opportunities for process improvements and scalability

In any engagement, you will center an empathetic and ethical approach. CalData works across all departments in the state, with data that reaches all Californians. You will find the problems and challenges we engage with needs the heart as much as the mind. You are able to connect with clients of varying levels of technical ability. You empathetically inquire about and understand the experiences of those impacted by data collection and its use.

About CalData division

CalData uses data to deliver better decisions, services, and outcomes for Californians.

As the central data team for the state, we empower people to use data. We build infrastructure, develop processes, and train people to better manage and use data. We want the state’s data use to be:

  • Efficient
  • Effective
  • Secure
  • Responsible

To help state departments use data well, we created California’s Data Strategy. We use a road analogy to illustrate the strategy. We must:

  • Build data roads
  • Craft rules of the road
  • Boost the drivers
