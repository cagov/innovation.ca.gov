The first and second cohorts include 21 employees. For 6 months, the Fellows will work on priority projects. They will make government more efficient, effective, and responsive to the public.

What the Fellows do

  • Spend 80% of their time on projects that improve efficiency, service delivery, and customer experience
  • Get training and hands-on help from the Office of Data and Innovation, CalAcademy, and partners in the private sector and academia
  • Work with executive sponsors and subject matter experts get measurable results through:
    • Applying product approaches
    • Using data
    • Conducting customer research

Why it matters

Governor Newsom launched this program as part of a broader executive order. Its goal is to make state government efficient, engaged, and effective. We chose the Fellows for their initiative, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

The Innovation Fellows Program isn’t like a traditional leadership program. We designed it to be a delivery engine. It develops leaders while creating real improvements that help Californians.

Looking ahead

The program will grow over time. New Fellows will join every 4 months. They will overlap with the earlier cohort so they can share knowledge and work together.

Meet the Fellows

The 21 inaugural Fellows started in 2025. They come from 9 agencies and 15 departments.

The second cohort of Fellows come from 11 agencies and 13 departments.
