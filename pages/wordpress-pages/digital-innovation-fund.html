The Digital Innovation Fund (DIF) is a path to partnership between your department and the Office of Digital Innovation (ODI).

Together, we will use human-centered design to identify problems and fund solutions that work for all Californians.

What makes a good DIF candidate

ODI looks for four qualities when reviewing DIF applications.

You want to improve your service

You acknowledge the need for improvement. You take an honest look at what’s not working now. You learn from failures to springboard to success. You’re ready to think through desired outcomes.  

You’re willing to collaborate

You see yourselves as co-designers of the service. Your team is willing to participate and collaborate with ODI to guarantee success that lasts.

You’re open to new solutions

You’re ready to discover, innovate, experiment. You’re focused on outcomes, rather than shortcuts. You’re flexible and willing to adopt new processes and technologies.

You’re committed to users first

You prioritize the needs of the end user over all else. You’re guided by user feedback and research. You want to remove the struggle from using the service.
