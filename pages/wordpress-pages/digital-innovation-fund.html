The Digital Innovation Fund (DIF) is a path to partnership between your department and the Office of Digital Innovation (ODI).

Together, we will use human-centered design to identify problems and fund solutions that improve services for all Californians.

What makes a good DIF candidate

ODI looks for four qualities when reviewing DIF applications.

1. Your service needs improvement

You have an awareness and curiosity about what’s not working now. You’re focused on desired outcomes and not solutions. You see opportunities to improve outcomes for Californians.

2. You’re excited to collaborate

You see yourselves as co-designers of the service. Your team is willing to participate and collaborate with ODI to guarantee success that lasts.

3. You’re open to new solutions

You’re ready to discover, innovate, and experiment. You learn from failures to springboard to success. You’re flexible and willing to adopt new processes and technologies.

4. You’re committed to putting users first

You prioritize the needs of the end user over all else. You’re guided by user feedback and research. You want to remove the struggle from using the service.
