The Innovation Community of Practice connects state employees who want to try new ways of doing things. It spotlights and amplifies the innovation happening throughout the state.

The Community promotes:

  • Sharing wins and lessons learned to inspire departments across the state
  • Learning through networking, training, and self-service resources
  • Enabling state staff to drive innovation and career advancement

The Community is open to all state employees. You don’t need to be a manager or a tech person to join. (After all, not all innovation involves technology.)

Email us to join

Attend a Teams meeting

Our quarterly meetings bring staff together from across the state. It’s an opportunity to collaborate with each other on innovation.

Meetings are a place for everyone to speak and share. We encourage conversation through activities like:

  • Breakout rooms
  • Digital whiteboards sessions
  • Discussion in the meeting chat
  • Time for questions

Each meeting includes a presentation about an innovation effort at a state department. Potential topics include:

  • How a process got better
  • Tools that made work easier
  • Ways we served the public better

It’s a great way to hear about what’s going on across the state.

We’ll be announcing the date and time of our next meeting soon.

Read our newsletter

Community members get a quarterly newsletter. You’ll find out about:

  • Innovation news from around the state
  • Upcoming training
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Innovation-related jobs on CalCareers

Connect on Teams

Community members get added to a Microsoft Teams channel. It’s a space to work and connect with other members any time you want.

Share your innovation

We want to hear how you’ve innovated! Your work could inspire another department.

Email InnovationCommunityofPractice@innovation.ca.gov if you’d like to share. You can present at a meeting or be featured in our newsletter.
