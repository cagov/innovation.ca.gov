There is a significant participation gap in some California state benefit programs. Many people who are eligible for these benefits haven’t signed up for them. 

The Benefits Recommender is a way to reach this audience and offer a compelling reason to click through and apply.

The Recommender is a lightweight addition to an existing webpage. It is typically added to the confirmation page for a popular benefit, like Unemployment Insurance.
