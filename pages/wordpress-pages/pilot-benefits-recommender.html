The Benefits Recommender is a new way to get people to apply for more California state benefits.

It meets people where they are on a benefits journey, and offers more aid they may not know about.

The Recommender is a lightweight addition to an existing webpage. It is typically added to the confirmation page for a popular benefit, like Unemployment Insurance.
