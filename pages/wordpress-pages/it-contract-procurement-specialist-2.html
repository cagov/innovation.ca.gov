As the IT Contract and Procurement Specialist, you will manage all IT contracts and procurement. You will be responsible for managing ODI’s full-service IT contracting program inclusive of contract management and contract budgeting within ODI. 

What you’ll do:

  • Manage IT procurement & contract intake, solicitation, and execution duties for ODI. 
  • Manage a full-service contracting program which includes contract management and budgeting.
  • Serve as ODI’s subject matter expert on IT services. 
  • Perform technical and analytical work. 
  • Act in a lead capacity.

Annual salary: $78,156 – $130,224 based on experience and qualifications

Benefits: The state offers many benefits, including pension, health benefits (including after retirement), life insurance, employee assistance program, and much more.

Last day to apply: Final filing date is 1/27/26

You will find additional information about the job in the Duty Statement.

Desirable qualifications

  • Knowledge of the State Contract Manual Volumes 1 and 2, State Administrative Manual, Government Code, Public Contract Code, and other governing laws/rules/regulations specific to state contracts
  • Knowledge and experience with executing IT service agreements of varying complexities and disciplines using solicitation methods such as RFP, RFO, RFQ, IFB, RFP, and NCB
  • Knowledge and experience with navigating user instructions for Statewide Contracts (LPA, SLP, NASPO, etc.)
  • Knowledge and experience with executing cloud service procurements 
  • Knowledge and experience with training program staff on Contract Management & procurement protocols/principles
  • Experience representing state departments in public with a tactful and professional demeanor
  • Experience with FI$Cal as a state buyer.
  • Experience with contract & procurement analysis 
  • Experience with gathering and analyzing facts, reasoning logically, drawing valid conclusions and making appropriate recommendations
  • Strong writing and analytical skills
  • Interpersonal skills 
  • Demonstrated public speaking experience as well as customer engagement 
  • Demonstrated ability to multi-task with changing priorities
  • Demonstrated ability to meet critical deadlines in a fast paced environment

About ODI

We deliver better services to Californians through technology and design. We do so through close partnership with other state departments. Our goal is to create programs and products that are more equitable and effective. We listen to Californians who use a program now or may do so tomorrow and learn about what they need.

We:

  • Build tools and products
  • Create and enhance digital services
  • Conduct research
  • Identify data solutions
  • Offer training

We are a cross-discipline staff with blended backgrounds in:

  • Design
  • Engineering
  • Product
  • Operations
  • Data
  • Research
  • Policy
  • Strategy

Diversity, equity, and inclusion at ODI

Diversity, equity, and inclusion help us deliver better government services to Californians.

  • Fostering diversity inspires better ideas and more innovative thinking.
