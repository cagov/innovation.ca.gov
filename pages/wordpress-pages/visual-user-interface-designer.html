We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Visual/UI Designer to join our UX Design team. Your job is to create designs that make it simple for people to interact with digital products. You enjoy creating visually appealing and compelling designs that enhance user experience and communicate our state partner’s brand effectively. You are well versed in collaborating within cross functional teams and working with stakeholders to understand, communicate, and deliver on their needs. You love to help others discover better ways of creating design for users. This is an opportunity to create user-focused, accessible products and a consistent user experience. You will use agile, iterative methods and work collaboratively with user researchers, content designers and engineers to design interfaces that are simple and easy to use.

Location: ODI staff reside throughout California. This position provides telework opportunities in accordance with agency telework policies.

Annual salary: $97,560 – $130,716 based on experience and qualifications

Benefits: The state offers many benefits, including pension, health benefits (including after retirement), life insurance, employee assistance program, and much more.

Last day to apply: Final filing date is January 12, 2024.

What you’ll do

  • Collaborate with partners to develop plans for comprehending, shaping, building, and overseeing visual identities.
  • Refine and improve rapidly by working together on prototypes with different levels of detail and accuracy.
  • Create clear, inclusive, accessible, and extensible visual styles and components in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.
  • Incorporate continuous feedback from users, peers, and stakeholders by leading design-focused usability and critique sessions.
  • Evangelize accessible and inclusive design practices throughout your work and partnerships.
  • Design intuitive and user-friendly interfaces for websites and applications, considering user experience and usability principles.
  • Test designs to see if they are working well for users and make improvements if needed. 
  • Work closely with cross-functional teams, including product managers, other designers, and engineers to understand design requirements and deliver solutions.
  • Create guidelines to make sure our designs look and feel consistent.

Desirable qualifications

In addition to evaluating each candidate’s relative ability, as demonstrated by quality and breadth of experience, the following factors will provide the basis for competitively evaluating each candidate.

We encourage you to apply regardless of whether you think you meet 100% of the desirable qualifications.

  • A portfolio of your previous projects that demonstrates your skills in visual and UI design, highlighting your creative process, ideation and problem-solving abilities, and attention to detail.
  • Proficient in design software, such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Figma, or Sketch.
  • Solid understanding and experience of design fundamentals: design principles, typography, color theory, and layout.
  • Experience developing visual design for brands, campaigns, and/or design systems.
  • Experience developing UX/UI design for digital products and services.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills to be able to talk to different stakeholders and understand each of their needs.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines.
  • Willingness to adapt to changes, be open to feedback, and revolving design requirements.
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to act independently and as part of a team.
  • Experience working within a multidisciplinary team of engineers, product managers, UX researchers, content designers, etc.
  • Experience in accessible and inclusive design.

About ODI

We deliver better services to Californians through technology and design. We do so through close partnership with other state departments. Our goal is to create programs and products that are more equitable and effective. We listen to Californians who use a program now or may do so tomorrow and learn about what they need.

We:

  • Build tools and products
  • Create and enhance digital services
  • Conduct research
  • Identify data solutions
  • Offer training

We are a cross-discipline staff with blended backgrounds in:

  • Design
  • Engineering
  • Product
  • Operations
  • Data
  • Research
  • Policy
  • Strategy

Diversity, equity, and inclusion at ODI

Diversity, equity, and inclusion help us deliver better government services to Californians.

  • Fostering diversity inspires better ideas and more innovative thinking.
  • Feeling included nurtures a shared sense of community. This drives us to take collective action on challenging problems.
